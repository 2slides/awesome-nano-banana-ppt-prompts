# Product launch deck in Apple-style minimalism

## Prompt Text

Build a product launch deck in Apple-style minimalism. Slides style can includes:
- Black background with product hero image only;
- Introducing [Product Name] big text;
- One killer feature per slide with stunning visual or short demo with well-designed images;
- Nice comparison table vs competitors;
- Clear visuals with all-to-action.
Use lots of white space, San Francisco font, black/white theme, apple gradient colours properly.
